Take Part

Active retirement living is so much more than a place to stay. It's a place to take part, a place to belong.

Our Life Engagement program is designed to promote resident wellness and independence while utilizing and valuing residents' strengths and successes within an environment that celebrates normalization, choice, empowerment and validation.

What you like to do, where you like to go, who you want to visit and much more. Here at Creekside, our mission is to help you enjoy the things you love and have more time for them.

We have a lot for you to do: Activities, socializing, exercising, reading or just plain relaxing. It's all waiting here for you. So come and join the neighborhood!

Creekside Retirement Community has various scheduled activities. The following are just a few examples:

  • Exercise Classes
  • Church Services
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Gardening
  • Educational opportunities
  • Bingo
  • Movies
  • Scheduled outings (Shopping, local tourist spots, etc...)
  • and more!

We look forward to seeing you here for all of our Special Events!
