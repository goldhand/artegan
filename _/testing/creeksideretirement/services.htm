Take Comfort

Comfort is a feeling that means you are home. At Creekside, we invite you to see what home looks like in beautiful Burlington, Washington.

Creekside takes pride in laying the foundation for your comfort by providing the essentials to put your mind at ease. We make delicious meals each day, take care of the little things like housekeeping and utilities that take your time and energy. We're here if you ever need assistance.

Here's what you can look forward to at Creekside:

Assisted Living

Assisted living at Creekside is designed for senior adults who wish to maintain their independent lifestyle but may need some additional assistance to do so.

Basic Services include:

  • 3 delicious meals served daily
  • 24-hour specially-trained on-site staff
  • Engaging activities
  • Apartment maintenance
  • Weekly housekeeping and linen service
  • Scheduled transportation
  • All utilities paid*
  • Emergency call systems in each apartment
  • Modern fire detection systems

In addition to the Basic Services we provide each of our residents, Creekside also offers Personalized Care Plans.

* - Phone and Cable TV may or may not be included. Call (360) 755-5550 for more information.
