Welcome to Laurel Parc at Bethany Village!

Laurel Parc at Bethany Village is a retirement community and senior assisted living community located in Portland, Oregon. As part of the charming Bethany Village neighborhood, we have gone to extraordinary lengths to create a retirement community where seniors enjoy life to the fullest.

Senior Care: Independent Living or Assisted Living?

Family members and friends gain peace of mind knowing that their loved ones reside where dignity and respect are cornerstones of this unique retirement community. Being a locally owned and operated business, Laurel Parc is committed to enhancing each resident's privacy, well-being, and quality of life.

Whether renting a spacious INDEPENDENT LIVING apartment at Laurel Parc or choosing the added care and comfort offered at The Vistas ASSISTED LIVING, Laurel Parc is the ideal place for you and those you care about. We make moving to Laurel Parc easy with our month to month rental arrangements and require no initial investment.

Laurel Parc is conveniently located 10 miles west of downtown Portland off highway 26. At Laurel Parc you will be able to stay connected as we are close to shopping, medical services and churches.

Welcome to Laurel Parc at Bethany Village, Northwest Portland’s newest and most distinctive Senior Living Community.
