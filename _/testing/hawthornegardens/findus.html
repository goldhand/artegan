How to Find Us

Hawthorne Gardens is located at:

2828 SE Taylor Street
Portland, OR 97214
971-222-0396

Directions from Downtown Portland

  • Cross the Hawthorne Bridge eastbound
  • Proceed on Hawthorne Blvd to 28th.
  • Turn left at 28th Ave
  • Turn right at Taylor St.
  • Hawthorne Gardens will be on your right

Directions from I-84 eastbound or westbound

  • Take the 39th St exit
  • Proceed south on 39th to Taylor St
  • Turn right on Taylor proceed westbound
  • Hawthorne Gardens will be on your left between 29th and 28th.
